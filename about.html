About PSquare Art Gallery

PSquare Art Gallery is a digital museum platform created as a Web Technology mini-project. Our goal is to provide visitors with an engaging and immersive experience of art, culture, and history through a modern web interface.

The gallery features curated collections of paintings, historical portraits, and modern artworks. We aim to make art accessible to everyone by combining traditional heritage with digital presentation.

This project demonstrates the use of HTML for structure, CSS for design, and JavaScript for dynamic interaction such as search and ticket calculation.
